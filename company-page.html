Our current breads:

Whole Wheat Bread is baked using flour that is almost entirely milled from whole wheat, as the name suggests. It is a type of brown bread and in some regions people call it 'Whole grain bread' or 'Wholemeal bread'



Sourdough Bread is made using wild lactobacillaceae and yeast. Lactic acid is what gives it the sour taste. It is speculated for sourdough bread to have originated in Egypt.


Baguette is a type of bread that is usually made with lean dough. What makes it unique is it's length and crisp. The baguette has a French origin and is often also called 'French Bread'.
